About Me

George is an undergradate geomatics engineer with a passion for UI/UX design. He received his Bachelor's degree in Geomatics Engineering from a reputable university and has since then worked in various geospatial technology companies, where he has gained extensive experience in GIS, remote sensing, and cartography. George's interest in UI/UX design was sparked by his experience working with geospatial software and tools that often have complex interfaces. He realized that the user experience could be significantly improved by applying UI/UX principles to geospatial software.To pursue his interest in UI/UX design, George took several courses in graphic design, user-centered design, and human-computer interaction. He has since then worked on several projects that involve designing intuitive and user-friendly interfaces for geospatial software.George's unique combination of geomatics engineering and UI/UX design skills has made him a valuable asset to any team working on geospatial projects. He is able to bridge the gap between the technical aspects of geospatial technology and the user experience, resulting in software that is not only functional but also easy to use and visually appealing.
